ABOUT US

Who We Are

Dev&Dash is an annual hackathon held by the School Of Engineering, St Aloysius. Marked by it's hype, the infamous code carnival, the presence of multiple MNCs, and the ultimate development challenge which created many entrepreneurs.

Our Mission

Our goal is to foster a community of builders and dreamers. We provide a platform to learn, network, and showcase talent to the world and to potential employers.
